About MarineStream

Learn about our mission, history, and the team behind MarineStream's innovation

Our Story

Founded in 2018, MarineStream emerged to tackle the growing challenge of biofouling in marine industries. We recognized the need for a comprehensive solution that could address regulatory requirements while leveraging modern technology to enhance efficiency and collaboration.

Built on a foundation of expertise in marine operations and advanced technology, MarineStream combines hardware innovation with blockchain capabilities to deliver a truly integrated approach to biofouling management.

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver comprehensive biofouling solutions that enhance compliance, transparency, collaboration, and real-time monitoring. We aim to transform how marine industries approach biofouling challenges by providing tools that not only meet today's requirements but anticipate tomorrow's needs.

Our Team
