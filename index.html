About MarineStream

MarineStream is dedicated to solving biofouling challenges through innovative hardware and blockchain technology, built on Rise-X's robust SaaS platform. Our mission is to deliver comprehensive biofouling solutions that enhance compliance, transparency, collaboration, and real-time monitoring.

  • Based on the DIANA Ecosystem Orchestration Platform (EOP)
  • Trusted by commercial vessels and naval fleets worldwide
  • Implementing transparency and traceability from down under to the world

MarineStream is a division of Franmarine, an Australian subsea and technology organisation renowned for supporting Defence with underwater services and support.
